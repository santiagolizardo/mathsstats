# {{ page.title }}

Typically used for equally likely events labelled 1 to <em>n</em>.

<dl>
    <dt>Range</dt>
    <dd>$$1, \ldots, n$$</dd>

    <dt>Probability mass function p(x)</dt>
    <dd>
    $$
    \frac{1}{n}
    $$
    </dd>

    <dt>Mean</dt>
    <dd>$$\frac{n + 1}{2}$$</dd>

    <dt>Variance</dt>
    <dd>$$\frac{n^2 - 1}{12}$$</dd>
</dl>
