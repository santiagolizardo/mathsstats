# {{ page.title }}

Typically used for counts of independently occurring events.

<dl>
    <dt>Notation</dt>
    <dd>$$\text{Poisson}(\mu)$$</dd>

    <dt>Range</dt>
    <dd>$$0, 1,  \ldots$$</dd>

    <dt>Probability mass function p(x)</dt>
    <dd>
    $$
    \frac{\mu^x e^{-\mu}}{x!}
    $$
    </dd>

    <dt>Mean</dt>
    <dd>$$\mu$$</dd>

    <dt>Variance</dt>
    <dd>$$\mu$$</dd>
</dl>
