## Definition

The **odds** of disease *D*, given exposure *E*, is

$$
\begin{equation}
OD(D|E) = \frac{P(D|E)}{P(\textit{not D}|E)}
\end{equation}
$$

The **odds ratio** is

$$
\begin{equation}
OR = \frac{P(D|E) \times P(\textit{not D}|{\textit{not E}})}{P(\textit{not D}|E) \times P(D|\textit{not E})}
\end{equation}
$$
